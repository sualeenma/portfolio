Santorini Logo

Having always wanted to visit Santorini, renowned for its white-washed charm, blue-domed buildings, and unforgettable sunsets, I decided to create a tourism logo for Santorini.

Ideation

Sketching

The initial phase of Santorini tourism logo design involved listing information related to this country through research. Considering the imagery of Santorini, I sketched various ideas and then illustrated the top three candidates to visualize them.
